Teleport Your NFT's into the Metaverse!

NFT Teleporter, is an online service which alows users to teleport their NFT collections into the Metaverse. Currently a proof of concept, our teleporter leverages IPFS's NFT Storage solutions, along with modern day 3D model file types, to allow NFT's to be viewable and interactable in VR (and soon AR).

Our service allows NFT creators to easily upload relevant meta data which you would usually associate with an NFT, along with digital 3D models and other exciting data that we turn into an IPFS hosted interactable experience automatically. These experiences are then made exclusively available to the wallet who owns the NFT.

We go one step further, and provide a unique storage flow which allows NFT creators to leverage this data at any time and pull their 3D models into their own custom experiences, built in softwares such as Unity and Unreal Engine.

This cross platform compatibility allows NFT creators to produce innovative collections quickly, which offer a generic but personalised experiences up front and the raw data needed to provide bespoke ones further down the road.
